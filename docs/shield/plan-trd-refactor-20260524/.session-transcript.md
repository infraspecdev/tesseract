## Product Context

### Problem
`/plan` produces a stories-first work-breakdown plus a `plan-architecture.md` companion. Industry convention is HLD → LLD; Shield skips the high-level-design layer. The new `/plan` should produce a TRD (= HLD + PM-lens milestones) with stories that link back to design sections. A separate LLD will be authored later per milestone.

### Users
Shield maintainers and end-users — engineers and tech leads generating plans for their own features. Specifically: the Shield maintainer and the engineers consuming `/implement` output downstream.

### Success criteria
Deferred — HLD/LLD is established industry practice (IEEE 1016 + Sommerville/Pressman). User judged that no novel success metric is needed beyond "matches industry HLD/LLD lineage."

### Why now
The current `plan-architecture.md` is closer to an ADR than an HLD; it lacks NFRs, viewpoint coverage, and explicit milestone derivation. The story breakdown has no traceability back to the design that justifies the work.
